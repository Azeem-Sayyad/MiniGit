Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how MiniGit collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

MiniGit collects basic user information such as name, email address, and login credentials to provide secure access to repositories and collaboration features.

2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used only to operate and improve the MiniGit platform, including authentication, repository management, and activity tracking.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Repository Privacy

Repositories created on MiniGit are accessible only to authorized users. Private projects remain confidential unless explicitly shared.

5. Cookies & Sessions

MiniGit uses session cookies strictly for authentication and maintaining secure user sessions. No tracking cookies are used.

6. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform implies acceptance of the revised policy.
